General Lewis Burwell "Chesty" Puller

Godfather of the United States Marine Corps


Here's a time line of Chesty Puller's life:



  • 1898 - born West Point Virginia.

  • 1918 - enlisted in the United States Marine Corps and attended recruit training at Marine Corps Recruit Depot, Parris Island, South Carolina.

  • 1919 - appointed 2nd lieutenant in the reserves.

  • 1924 - received his permanent commission.

  • 1955 - after suffering a stroke General Puller retired from the USMC.

  • 1971 - died Hampton Virginia.


"Old Breed? New Breed? There's not a damn bit of difference as long as it's the Marine breed."
~Chesty Puller
